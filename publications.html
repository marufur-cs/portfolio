[2025] Communication Lower Bounds and Algorithms for Sketching with Random Dense Matrices
Hussam Al Daas, Grey Ballard, Laura Grigori, Md Taufique Hussain, Suraj Kumar, Mohammad Marufur Rahman, Kathryn Rouse
This paper addresses the communication bottlenecks of sketching large matrices in distributed-memory environments. The authors establish communication lower bounds, prove their tightness with optimal algorithms, and extend these bounds to Nyström approximation. Evaluations on CPU/GPU supercomputers demonstrate the scalability of their proposed parallel strategies.
Paper link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20966

[2025] Catastrophic Forgetting in Kolmogorov-Arnold Networks
Mohammad Marufur Rahman, Guanchu Wang, Kaixiong Zhou, Minghan Chen, Fan Yang
Published in AAAI 2026
This study evaluates KANs for continual learning. While KANs resist forgetting in low-dimensional, algorithmic tasks due to localized activations, they remain vulnerable in high-dimensional domains like vision and language. The authors introduce KAN-LoRA to improve fine-tuning and provide a theoretical framework linking forgetting to activation overlap.
Paper link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.12828
