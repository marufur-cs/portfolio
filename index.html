About Me

Hi, I'm Mohammad Marufur Rahman, a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at University of Texas at Dallas (UTD). I work in Human Language Technology Research Institute (HLTRI) under the supervision of Dr. Sanda M. Harabagiu. My research primarily focuses on the areas of large language models, natural language processing, and trustworthy AI.

Prior joining UTD, I completed my master's degree in Computer Science at Wake Forest University, where I was advised by Dr. Grey Ballard. Before that, I earned my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Khulna University of Engineering and Technology (KUET). Before starting graduate studies, I worked as a senior lecturer at Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST) in Bangladesh, where I taught various courses in computer science and supervised undergraduate research projects.
